Our Story

Made with Passion

Sri Selvi Store Pte Ltd is a trusted neighbourhood grocery and convenience store located at 408 Serangoon Ave 1, Sunshine Ville, Singapore. We stock a wide range of everyday essentials, fresh produce, and pantry staples to keep your home well-supplied. Whether you need a quick top-up or a full grocery run, we are here to serve the local community every single day.

Conveniently situated in the heart of Serangoon, we pride ourselves on being accessible and consistent — opening our doors at 8:30 AM and staying open until 10:00 PM daily. As a local Indian grocery store, we also carry a curated selection of South Asian ingredients, spices, and specialty items that cater to the diverse tastes of our neighbourhood customers.
